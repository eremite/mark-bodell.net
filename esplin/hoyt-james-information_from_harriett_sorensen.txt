# Information from the records of Harriett B. Sorensen, Orderville, Utah

James Hoyt was the son of James Hoyt and Hannah Elliot

Born 1775 Concord, New Hampshire

Married Beulah Sabin Born 11 June 1786 New York state

They had 10 children; Pauline, Hyrum, Timothy, Henry, Pike, Lucy, Homer, Mary,
Harriett, Angeline and Israel. All born in New York state.
