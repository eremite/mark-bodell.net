THE WEBSTER FAMILY. Henry Webster and his wife Ann Rigby Webster and eleven
children made their home in St. Louis for a short time. They were natives of
England. Lydia, who was 22 years of age, married James S. Brooks of St. Louis
and remained there for several years.
